## Plan user story

**As a** bum daily-driver user, **I want to** complete GPT-5.6 turns and Grok↔GPT-5.6 switches reliably, **so that** I can keep coding in one CLI without a restart.

<objective>
Prevent foreign provider encrypted reasoning from reaching Codex after a true model-provider transition while keeping useful full history and same-provider Codex continuity.

Purpose: close the OPS-05 decrypt/verify 400 without deleting ordinary conversation context.
Output: a local field-level sanitizer, pure semantic tests, and outbound request-capture coverage.
</objective>

## Locked decisions honored

- Sanitize only on a true provider change and only the provider-scoped encrypted payload; preserve ordinary messages, tool data, summaries, and non-encrypted reasoning.
- Same-provider Codex continuity is a positive requirement, not an accidental consequence of test setup.
- `model_switch::apply` is the shared model-transition entry point; preserve encrypted payload only when the request/response origin is known to match the active target provider. Unknown origin is conservative and clears the encrypted field rather than silently treating it as same-provider continuity.
- Bum session identity remains primary. Optional Codex request headers are metadata handled in the dependent trusted-shell plan.
- Phase 11 reasoning capability policy and Phase 12 WebSocket/tool identity work are unchanged.
